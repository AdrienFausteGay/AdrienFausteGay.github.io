Accredited Observer at COP 27

The ENS gave me the opportunity to go to COP 27 in November 2022 in Sharm-El-Sheik on the southern tip of Sinai. I had access to almost all of the blue zone, the diplomatic area where the negotiations take place. This trip deeply marked me with the nature of the event and the meetings I made. Career choice is an important element. I think scientists have a responsibility to contribute, a role to play and real added value to bring to these issues. I brought a camera with me to take pictures and record some of my meetings. We were invited to talk about our presence at COP 27 at the French School in Sharm-El_Sheik. Finally, we were able to interact with various journalists, resulting in the publication of several articles.

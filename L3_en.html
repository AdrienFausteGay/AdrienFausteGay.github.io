Instrumentation of new soil columns

Work carried out as part of my L3 internship. ENS has a laboratory called Ecotron located near Nemours, France. This laboratory is able to simulate various natural environments to study the influence of environmental stress on various animals and plants. I developed a system to automatically monitor the water and CO2 levels in various places of soil columns over several days.
